The Windy City: Chicago

Skyscrapers and River Tours

Chicago is a dream destination for anyone who appreciates grand engineering. The city's skyline is filled with iconic structures like the Willis Tower and the John Hancock Center. I’ve always wanted to take one of their famous architecture boat tours along the Chicago River to see these masterpieces from the water and learn the history behind the birthplace of the skyscraper.

Meeting "The Spirit" at the United Center

As a basketball enthusiast, no trip to Chicago would be complete without a pilgrimage to the United Center. Seeing the legendary statue of Michael Jordan, known as "The Spirit," is a major bucket list item. Standing before the bronze tribute to the greatest to ever play the game is something every fan needs to experience in person.

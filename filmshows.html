Binge-Worthy Series
Breaking Bad Follow the transformation of Walter White from teacher to kingpin.
Prison Break An innocent man goes to prison to break out his brother on death row by using a map of the facility tattooed across his body.
Ozark A financial advisor drags his family from Chicago to the Missouri Ozarks to launder $500 million for a drug cartel to keep them all alive.
Better Call Saul The tragic and comedic transformation of Jimmy McGill as he descends into the world of crime to become the flamboyant lawyer Saul Goodman.
The Walking Dead In the aftermath of a zombie apocalypse, survivors struggle to stay alive against both the undead and the ruthless humans who remain.
Narcos A gritty, fact-based chronicle of the rise and fall of Pablo Escobar and the Medellín Drug Cartel, following the bloody battle between the billionaire kingpin and the DEA agents tasked with bringing him down.

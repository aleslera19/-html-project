Tracing Our Roots: Spain

Choosing Spain as a dream destination feels more like a history trip than just a vacation. Since Spain colonized the Philippines for over 300 years, I've always been fascinated by how much of our own culture actually comes from theirs. From the food we eat to the way our streets are designed, the connection is everywhere.

I really want to visit to see those similarities in person. I imagine walking through Spanish plazas and seeing the architecture that inspired places like Intramuros or Vigan back home. It would be amazing to experience the source of so many of our traditions—the language, the faith, and even the festivals—and see how they’ve evolved differently over time. It feels like a journey to find a piece of our own identity.

A journey to see the roots of Filipino culture.
